# DigitalPiano

    Buttons can be clicked using the cursor 
    
    or
    
    They can be directly pressed by your keyboard
    
       w: C#   e: D#       t: F#   y: G#  u: A#         o: C1#   p: D1#
    a: C , s: D , d: E , f: F , g: G , h: A , j: B , k: C1 , l: D1 , ?:E1 , ?:F1
